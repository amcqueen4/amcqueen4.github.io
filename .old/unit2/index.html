Math? Whats that?

"Mathematics: the science of structure, order, and relation that has evolved from elemental practices of counting, measuring, and describing the shapes of objects."
To put it in other words, mathematics is the most important field of study. All fields of study implement core reasoning and study from mathematics.

Why would anyone need it?

Every modern problem requires mathematics to be described and solved. Consider a basic situation such as the profit margin from a business: If a company sells a doodad for five dollars, but it costs an initial twenty dollars to start operation, how would you represent this situation?
